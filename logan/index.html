The Logan Future

With 40 years of history behind us, find out how we see the future of Logan Group...

Originally a technical drawer, Derek has long been inspired by great design and his passion for meticulous attention to detail. Under his expert and careful guidance, Logan Homes has gone on to build between five and ten stunning houses a year at a series of developments up and down the country.

In 2011, Philip Gilbey brought his 20-years+ of experience in the property industry, to the helm of Logan Group. Together, Derek and Philip have a vision for the future to build on the company's existing heritage and core values, and to continue building stunning and superior homes.

Logan Group is committed to building stunning 5* unique homes, designed entirely for the landscape they inhabit. The end result? Something truly rare... new homes that enhance existing villages.

Our approach is simple. Building homes that fit seamlessly into their location. On the day of completion, our properties are designed to look as though they have been there for years. How do we ensure we achieve this? Quite simply, we only design a house once we know exactly where it will be built, allowing us to ensure that the house perfectly fits with the character of the buildings next to which it will sit.

Our mission statement is simple - to build brand new, period style properties which are unsurpassable in quality, matchless in character and effortlessly timeless.

With 40 years of home building to our name, our knowledge and experience is second to none, and it is this that equips us so well whatever the next 40 years brings
